Problems

The cross-border team's product managers have reported a high volume of merchant inquiries about the enterprise verification process, leading to increased customer support workload.

Solutions

Based on an analysis of issues in the original IA and flow, as well as potential user confusion, I designed an intuitive and self-service verification process that reduced merchant confusion.

Project Goals

How might we make the enterprise verification process clearer, more intuitive, and self-serviceable to reduce merchant confusion and support inquiries?

Launch Results

After launch, we saw a reduction in verification-related inquiries from merchants, with fewer calls and emails to customer support, resulted in higher satisfaction from both customers and support staff.

Design Process

I analysed user feedback and conducted a heuristic evaluation to identify pain points. Then I restructured the flow, streamlined the interaction and visual design to enhance usability. Lastly, I worked closely with developers to ensure smooth implementation.

Heuristic Evaluation

I created a heuristic evaluation diagram that focused on identifying areas where the design violated established usability principles and where improvements could be made.

IA & Flow Optimisation

I restructured the flow into a clear, step-by-step format, making it easier to navigate.

Challenges

We faced several challenges when implementing OCR functionality and draft features, mainly due to tight timelines and the complexity of integrating these advanced features into the existing system.

Key Pages

Key Learning

In fast-paced projects, it's rarely possible to implement all design ideas in a single sprint. As designers, it's important to stay focused on priorities, keep close communication with the team, and make sure the most critical features are delivered quickly and to a high standard.

Future Considerations

In the next iteration, we introduced an FAQ section, allowing users to easily find answers to common questions about enterprise verification on the page, further reducing the workload for customer support.
