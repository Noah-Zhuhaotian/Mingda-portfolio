Problems

The product team noticed that user online loan applications were below expectations, and the conversion rate from online appointments to in-person deposits was low.

Pain Points & Solutions

I analysed user pain points, designed the information architecture, and created an end-to-end online car loan application process, leveraging the company's strengths to drive seamless online-to-offline conversion.

Business Goals

While solving user pain points, I helped the product meet business goals—boosting loan applications and car orders.

How might we create a seamless, transparent, and fully digital car purchase and loan experience while increasing loan application rates and deposit conversions?

Project Launch Results

During development, I set up tracking on the webpages and CTA buttons. After the website update, CTA clicks and loan conversion rates improved.

Design Process

Considering the extensive work involved in redesigning the online car loan process, I divided the project into three phases of design and development.

User Journey

To identify pain points in the car loan process, I collaborated with the product manager and mapped out a user journey, revealing business opportunities and potential solutions for the company.

Information Architecture

Based on the user journey and analysis, I designed the IA and key pages to guide users through selecting a car, paying a deposit online, and completing the loan signing process via the app at the dealership.

Project Management

As the project manager, I structured each phase effectively, managing timelines while balancing design work with development-ready content, overseeing progress, and driving testing and optimisation.

Challenges

SAIC's partner dealers had independent pricing and contract systems, making a fully seamless online process impossible.

Future Considerations

In the future, I would explore ways to better integrate dealership salespeople into the loan experience. Providing them with simple tools (like a loan comparison sheet or a short training video) could boost their confidence in recommending our platform and improve offline conversions.
