Inside No.9 Infographic: A Fan Tribute

Ideation

Illustration

Prototyping

Testing

Overview

Inside No. 9 is a British dark comedy anthology series renowned for its clever writing, dark humour, and unexpected twists. This infographic showcases my fan journey and celebrates the series' legacy, aiming to introduce it to a wider audience.

Concept Ideation

I drew inspiration from the show's authentic settings, hoping to integrate content into these scenes to immerse viewers in its environment.

Testing and Iteration

I tested these concepts with several of my friends who had never heard of this show and then got insights for improvements.

Colours and Shapes

I selected a black background paired with a high-contrast colour scheme to reflect the dark comedy theme, and I used doors as the opening and closing elements to visually represent Inside No. 9 while paying homage to its primarily indoor settings.

Illustration Style

To keep consistency, I desaturated and cropped images of the creators and stills from the show, then completed the visual elements using Figma.

Interactivity and Animations

I used many animations to add levels to the information and carefully picked the right effects for each element.

Data Visualisation

For data-heavy awards and ratings sections, I used simple charts and animations to reduce reading fatigue.

Final Presentation

I invited users to step through the door of Room No.9 and begin their journey of exploring the infographic.
