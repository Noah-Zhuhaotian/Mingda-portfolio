I'm originally from Shanghai but now live in New Zealand. After graduating from university, I taught myself UI design and gradually transitioned into UX and product design. I approach design challenges by deeply understanding user needs, questioning assumptions, and refining solutions until they truly make sense. I drive implementation, track data, and iterate to improve outcomes.

Last year, I moved to New Zealand to further my studies in User Experience Design at VUW. I've settled in and love the rich cultural and artistic vibe here.

I am practical and productive, specialising in creating simple yet effective designs. My legal background gives me a strong sense of logic and structure, which helps me analyse and solve problems efficiently. My natural creativity and eagerness to keep learning make my work solid and engaging.
